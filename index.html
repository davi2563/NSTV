Who is Neversometimes TV?

Laura is a social media personality best known for her near three year weight loss journey. Her journey got a jump start after she moved back in with her parents after her divorce. While she was semi-isolated from her friends and regular social scene, she began to focus on her weight loss. She started by counting calories with MyFitnessPal. From there she focused on lowering her calories and incorporating fitness into her everyday routine. Fast-forward two and half years and she's lost 120 pounds and has gained a massive online following. After her journey found the spotlight, she started a YouTube channel to document her life with videos about her eating habits, how weight loss has changed her life and To follow her progress, you can click on the tabs on the side to see her Instagram, YouTube and Twitter accounts.
